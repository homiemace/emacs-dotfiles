* Big Questions

** What is this?

Packets are small chunks of data sent over a network.

** Why is this important?

Because most network communication happens by breaking data into packets.

** When will I need this?

When I want to understand how devices communicate over a network.

** How does it work?

A message is broken up into small chunks called packets. Each packet contains:

- Header: Metadata like where it came from, where it is going, and how it should be handled.
- Payload: The actual data.
